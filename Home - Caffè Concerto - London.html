Caffe Concerto is a Italian restaurant with branches in Central London as well as Birmingham and the Middle East. With a dedication to fine food, excellence in service and a love of the fine art of patisserie and coffee, Caffe Concerto is proud to serve its customers surrounded by beautiful decor and live music at selected venues. 


Caffe Concerto is also the renowned for its wedding cakes and celebration cakes. Please call our cake studio on: 0207 494 6837 to book a consultation at our showroom on 28 South Molton Street, London, W1K 5RF. Our advisor will be happy to talk you through your options as well as show you the great work created by our talented patisserie chefs at our head kitchen located in Park Royal.
